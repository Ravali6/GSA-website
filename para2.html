Graduate Student Association

We love to unite

The Graduate Student Association (GSA) is an organization made up of all graduate students at New Mexico Tech. The GSA represents graduate students on policy-making committees, including Faculty Council, Graduate Council, Institute Senate, and the Student Association. We work with the administration to address issues relating to student life at Tech. We award travel grants for graduate students to present research at professional meetings. We also provide some social activities for graduate students and their families.

All degree seeking graduate students are members of the GSA, and are invited to participate in all GSA committees, meetings, and activities and events. Decisions are made by the GSA Council, which is made up of representatives from each graduate department. Departmental representation is based on the number of graduate students in the department. Each department is responsible for electing their own representatives. Officers are elected from the GSA Council by Council members.

Meetings are open to all graduate students, and we welcome your input. Meeting announcements are sent through the GSA mailing list. To subscribe to the GSA email list simply send a blank email with the subject subscribe to GSA email list to: gsa@npe.nmt.edu

GSA Committees

The GSA constitution needs a makeover. If you are interested contact the GSA.

Events Committee

A fairly new addition to GSA events is the annual Interdisciplinary Symposium which highlights a differnt topic each year. If you are interested in volunteering for this committee or have ideas for topics/guest speakers/etc then please contact the GSA. Previous topics include Astrobiology any Biomedical.

Spring Picnic Committee

Each spring the GSA hosts a Spring Picnic for the graduate students.  If you are interested in helping plan this event, please contact the GSA.

Judicial Committee

Three members are necessary to complete the judicial committee. Duties include:

  • To determine questions of constitutionality and adherence to this document.
  • To advise the Council as requested.
  • To maintain records of Judiciary Oversight Board decisions.
  • Select a Judiciary Oversight Board Chair who will conduct meetings and act as the voice of the Board.

Budget Committee

Generally, this committee meets once near the end of the academic year to set the budget for the following year. If you would like to volunteer to be on this committee or would have budgetary concerns to bring up with the GSA please contact the GSA. If you have budgetary concerns, you can request to set up a time to speak at a regular GSA meeting.

Travel Grant Review Committee

The Travel Grants Review Committee reviews the travel grant applications for a specific travel period. Being a part of the Travel Grants Committee is a one-time commitment that consists of reading all of the travel grants for that travel period. If you are interested in being on the Travel Grants Review Committee, you may contact the GSA Grants Officer at gsa@nmt.edu.
